! Adposition lexicon
! ===================

! First come the 3 continuation lexica, the division is based on Nickel
! and should probably be revised. Then comes the adpositions themselves.
! The uninflecting ones are pointed to the 3 tag lexica, the Px ones to the
! Px lexica in sme-lex.txt and closed-sme-lex.txt.

! According to Bergsland, Southern Sami has two groups of adpositions, 
! postpositions (such as 'sistie'), with a genitive complement, and combined 
! pre- and postpositions, such as 'rastah'. Here, the former are marked 
! +Po and the latter +Adp.

! Tagging sublexica
! -----------------

LEXICON Ad ! Here, we do not know wehter they are +Pr or +Po
+Po:0 FINAL1 ;
+Pr:0 FINAL1 ;

LEXICON Postp
+Po:0 FINAL1 ;

LEXICON Prep
+Pr:0 FINAL1 ;
